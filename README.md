## NIM-GAME

Singleplayer or multiplayer game mode available

Rules : 
- Each player can pick up from one pile. 
- The player can only take one or two tokens per turn. 
- DO NOT select a row that doesn't exist otherwise your turn may be skipped or the program may end. 
- The winner is the player who makes the last move. 

May the best nimmer win! >:)
